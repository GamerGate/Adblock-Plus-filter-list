Adblock-Plus-filter-list

This is the GamerGate Adblock Plus filter list.

It's an Adblock Plus compatible version of the GamerGate boycott list.

Once installed, it will automatically block all domains on the boycott list.

You can install it by clicking here.

You'll need an Adblock Plus compatible extension installed on your browser, first. These include Adblock Plus, Adblock Edge, and µBlock. Adblock Plus is available for Firefox, SeaMonkey, Chrome, Chromium, Opera, Safari, Internet Explorer, and Android. Adblock Edge is available for Firefox and SeaMonkey. µBlock is available for Chrome, Chromium, and Opera.
